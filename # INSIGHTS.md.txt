# INSIGHTS.md

## 1. Dropout Risk in Python Track (Q2)
Python has a fail rate >40%. Altrodav should introduce
checkpoint assessments or peer mentoring before Level 40
to reduce early dropouts.

## 2. Low Engagement — Only 1 Student Has a 7-Day Streak (Q3)
Platform engagement is critically low. Introducing daily
streaks, gamification badges, or reminder notifications
could improve retention significantly.

## 3. QIS College Needs Targeted Support (Q1)
QIS students take longer to reach Level 50 in Aptitude.
College-specific intervention programs or fast-track
modules could close this gap.

## 4. Placement Rate Disparity Across Colleges (Q5)
Some colleges show 100% placement while others lag behind.
Altrodav should partner more deeply with high-performing
colleges and diagnose what skill gaps exist at lower-ranked ones.
